Sponsored by the crew at Sample Brew and recorded in the storied Bakehouse studios of Abbotsford, Albert Salt: Live at the Bakehouse is an intimate document of one of Melbourne undergroundâ€™s most dynamic young artists. A musical Multicam experience strings together two of Albert Salt's albums including 'Wilson Street and Hamilton'. Available on spotify. Personally, I held one of the dynamic camera's and edited every video to completion along with my producer and Artist, Andy McCallum and Albert Salt. The collection of video's can be found on YouTube.





Short Film 'Pick Up' was created by a talented group of students at SAE Qantm and completed a trip around the world at different film festivals until finally winning a prize for 'Best Aus youth film makers' at Dungog FF. During this project I was the main editor and worked closely with my Director and Producer Ana Carolina Lima and Shaki Prasanna . Other Film festivals this short film played at include:
Southern Sunset Undergraduate FF
Footprints FF
Susquehannock FF
Singapore International FF





Glass Plains by Albert Salt was released on Spotify and gained instant attention from the likes of Sin radio and Triple J and even got a play on the popular TV show 'The Block'. Along with the release of the song came the Glass plains video clip, our artistic follow up to 'Live at the bakehouse'. For this particular project I was Writer, Producer, AC and Editor. After receiving a positive review from hhhhappy, Albert and I decided to continue working together to produce another new video clip for his most recent release, Evaline. Video clip will be out soon.
